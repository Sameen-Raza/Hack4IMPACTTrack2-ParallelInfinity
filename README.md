## Domain
**Cybersecurity for Small & Medium Businesses (SMBs)**

---

## Problem Statement

400M+ small businesses worldwide are prime cyberattack targets, yet cannot afford or navigate enterprise security tools. 
SMB owners have no way to know if their website is blacklisted, their SSL has lapsed, 
or their email has been breached — until it's too late.
CyberShield solves this with a real-time, no-login security scanner that assesses URLs and emails, 
scores risk intelligently, and delivers plain-English action steps.

---

## Our Solution — CyberShield

CyberShield gives any SMB owner a **full security assessment in under
60 seconds** — no login, no installation, no jargon.

**Flow:**
📥 Input (URL/Email) → 🔍 Scan → 📊 Risk Score → ✅ Action Plan → 📤 PDF Export
